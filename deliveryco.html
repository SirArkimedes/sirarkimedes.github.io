Description

As a driver for the leading transporting company in the world your job is to make sure the packages get to where they belong. This is no simple task, as the routes are full of obstacles. Avoid the obstacles to gain piles of coins so you can unlock more vehicles with different and special abilities. Only one company can be number 1. Do you have what it takes?


This app competed for the SkillsUSA Interactive Application and Game Design contest. My group, me and three others, took this app to our state competition and placed first. A few months later we got to go to Louisville to participate in the national event and placed 5th out of about 15 teams.

The last image you see in the screenshots is my very first version of the project when I began to experiment with the frameworks.
